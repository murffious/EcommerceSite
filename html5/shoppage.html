About

Making bright, joyous and meaningful Jewish art.

M2V was born when three abounding rivers joined forces to flow as one!

Our first collection set out what became M2V's distinct style - a mix of bohemian and minimalist elements, feminine and masculine energy and the ambition to reconcile our reverence for traditions and ethnicity with a contemporary look. We create designs for women and men who carry the spirit of travelers, value personal experience and sensing over borrowed truths, and perceive fashion as another expression of human's relentless creativity.

Light and joy in life have been the central message of much of the Hassidic tradition, and this is the feeling I try to convey in my Judaica art and painting. I love the bright light and colours of the Land of Israel, and my paintings, I hope, evoke some of that spiritual energy. When you buy one of my artworks, you are bringing some of this energy into your home!

To measure yourself:

  Bust
Wrap a soft tape measure around your bust. It should wrap around the fullest part of your bust. The tape should be straight and parallel with the floor.
  Waist
Wrap the tape measure around your natural waist. Keep it parallel to the floor. Do not hold your breath or suck your stomach in. Hold your body erect in a comfortable standing position to get an accurate measurement. Be sure you don't draw it too tight.
  Inseam
Measure the length of the inner leg, from the top of the thigh to the ankle.
  Hips
Wrap the tape measure around the fullest part of your hips and buttocks. This is usually located 7 to 9 inches below your natural waist. Keep the tape measure parallel to the floor.
